Mumbai City Cuisine

Mumbai is a culinary paradise with a diverse range of flavors. Here are some must-try dishes and food experience in the city:

Pav Bhaji

A popular street food dish consisting of a spicy vegetable curry (bhaji) served with sofr bread rolls (pav).

Vada Pav

Known as the "Indian Burger", vada pav consists of a sipcy potato fritter (vada) placed inside a pav.

Bhel Puri

A delightful combination of puffed rice, sev, chutneys, and various ingredients, creating a tasty snack.
