Interesting Facts About Mumbai City

Dabbawalas

Mumbai's dabbawalas are known for their efficient lunchbox delivery system, with a Six Sigma certificate of accuracy.

Bollywood

Mumbai is the heart of the Indian film industry, commonly known as Bollywood, producing numerous films each year.

Elephanta Caves

The Elephanta Caves, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, feature rock-cut caves dedicated to Hindu and Buddhist deities.
